Our Mission

SGL Labs is committed to protecting the environment and promoting sustainability within the diamond and precious metals industry. Through EcoAssure, we aim to minimise carbon footprints, enhance environmental responsibility and support a greener future. By integrating sustainable practices throughout our supply chain, reducing waste and conserving energy, we strive to create a lasting positive impact on the planet.

Eco-Friendly

Supporting the protection of wildlife and nature for a greener tomorrow.

Zero Waste

Encouraging responsible production that reduces harm and eliminates waste.

Carbon Neutral

Lowering carbon emissions across every stage of jewellery manufacturing.

Impact of Gold Mining

Gold mining contributes to severe environmental damage and societal harm. Some key issues include:

Human Rights

Poor working conditions and human rights violations.

Waste

Mining operations generate enormous waste that damages ecosystems.

Use of Water

High water consumption that disrupts natural water resources.

Recycled Gold Certification

EcoAssure verifies the authenticity of recycled gold in jewellery with SGL’s trusted certification. This ensures your pieces support ethical sourcing and environmental responsibility.
