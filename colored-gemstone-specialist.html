Career at SGL often starts in our classroom.

Colored Gemstone Professional is a course that elaborates on the expertise and knowledge for gem enthusiasts and enables them to identify, grade, and value gemstones based on international standards. The course is structured as per the international colored stone industry, which will enable the candidate to easily relate to the current market dynamics and terminologies for an allround grasp over the subject.

Overview of the course

    Introduction to basic terms in gemology
    Learn to operate various gemological instruments to identify and assess them
    Study about Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Tanzanite, Quartz, Peridot, Turquoise, Tourmaline and more
    Treatments and Synthetics carried out for the beautification of the gemstones

Topics Covered

Treatment & processes

Learn about various gemstones and their significance

Quality factors

Evaluation on the basis of various characteristics of a gem

Treatments & processes

Methods to make a gem look more appealing

Instruments & equipment

A guide to understand the usage of the devices for gemstone assessment
