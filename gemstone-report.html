Lab Policy Related to Lab-Grown Diamond Certification

Access to Data

SGL lab-grown Diamond reports are freely available to view, verify and download. All the SGL lab-grown certification data is maintained systematically and can be presented on demand as a right to consumer perusal limited to the certificate.

Confidence

Providing professional lab-grown diamond identification and grading services backed with the latest technology SGL is at the front end of the laboratory-grown diamond detection scenario. SGL’s specialised services and laboratory-grown detection instruments help in the easy identification of natural diamonds.

Security Features

UV text visible under UV/Blacklight, Secured 3D holograms and a special paper make the SGL lab-grown certificate a document that cannot be tampered with or easily duplicated. Our diamond sealing also displays many tamper-evident features to provide peace of mind.

Client Anonymity

At SGL, we go above and beyond to maintain client confidentiality. We use a system of barcodes and software modules to ensure that the grading and processing of diamonds and their certificates are done without any knowledge of the client’s details. This allows us to provide our clients with unbiased grading reports.
