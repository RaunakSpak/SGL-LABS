About Us

Global Excellence In Diamond Certification

Founded in 2007, SGL is a British diamond certification & authentication laboratory headquartered in London. As one of the fastest-growing international laboratories, we operate in 5 countries with 23 locations & 198 geo-footprints worldwide. Our commitment to excellence ensures accurate & reliable certification of both Natural & Lab-Grown Diamonds,setting industry benchmarks.

Precision, Integrity & Innovation

At SGL, every diamond undergoes stringent testing using state-of-the-art analytical instruments to ensure authenticity and quality. Our expert gemologists, with deep scientific and gemmological knowledge, conduct assessments with unmatched precision. We take pride in maintaining transparency and trust in every certification,reinforcing the credibility of our reports.

Advancing Awareness & Education

Beyond certification, SGL actively contributes to global awareness and education in the diamond industry. Through extensive research, workshops and collaborations, we provide valuable insights into diamond classification, identification and treatments. Our mission is to empower jewellers, retailers and consumers with knowledge they can trust.
