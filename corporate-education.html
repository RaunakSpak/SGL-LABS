In this ever-evolving market, your staff must know the basics of diamonds and gemstones. We at SGL aim to provide you with the best knowledge for your counter sales staff, Q.C. specialists, assorters, floor managers, etc. Our highly trained faculty will arrange for short batches which will cover a variety of topics related to diamond 4Cs, gemstone basics, and sales techniques.

These short courses will benefit manufacturing staff by enabling them to make better and accurate buying decisions. They will also aid in a better selection of goods to be fit in JEWELLERY.

Jewelers will see a marked difference in the performance of their staff as these courses cover confidence building, mock conversations between client and counter staff, etc. The counter sales staff will also be in a better position to explain the product to their client, thus helping them to make an informed decision.
